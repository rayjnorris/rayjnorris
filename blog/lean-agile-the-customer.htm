LEAN AGILE - the customer

March 21, 2017

The CUSTOMER is an essential partner to the Lean Agile practice ...


Lean Agile is a ‘Customer-Centric Approach’ to Software Development which requires active and considerable customer participation until the product is complete and even after. So, who is this 'customer'?


Understanding who our real customer is is essential to having the right people on our team. Each person who will use our product may have a different role (actor roles) and therefore different needs from our product. So, really, we could have many 'customer actors', each of which should help drive the product solution until delivery (and even after). It's possible that each 'actor role' (customer role) could be so different from each other, but still using the same automation solution, that we decide to 'chunk' the solution. In fact, that is quite common.
